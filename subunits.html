NCT U

The first NCT's sub-unit debuted on April 9, 2016 with the single "The 7th Sense". The "U" stands for United. Unity from every combination of NCT members. So, this isn't a fixed sub-unit. The number of members changes depending on which NCT active member fits the concept of the song each comeback.

NCT 127

The first fixed and second overall sub-unit of NCT. The number "127" is the longitude coordinate of the city of Seoul. They debuted on July 7, 2016 with the single "Firetruck". The original lineup consists of 10 members: Taeil, Taeyong, Yuta, Johnny, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Winwin, Jungwoo, Mark, and Haechan.

NCT DREAM

Third sub-unit of NCT. NCT Dream has been internationally recognised as among the most notable teen-aged artists of their time. The group debuted on August 25, 2016 with the single "Chewing Gum", with a lineup consisting of seven members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle, and Jisung.

WAYV

(Chinese: 威神V; WēiShén V) is the fourth sub-unit and China-based unit of NCT managed by SM Ent.'s Chinese sub-label, Label V. The group debuted on January 17, 2019, with their digital extended play The Vision, and is composed of 7 members: Kun, Ten, Winwin, Lucas, Xiaojun, Hendery and Yangyang.
