NCT 2020 YearParty

NCT (Korean: 엔시티, an acronym for Neo Culture Technology) is a South Korean boy group formed by SM Entertainment. The name NCT is a term to describe group's concept of having an unlimited number of members divided multiple sub-units. Currently, NCT has 23 members and 4 sub-units.
